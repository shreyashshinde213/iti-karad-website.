Our Trades (Courses)

List of NCVT/SCVT Certified Trades offered at ITI Karad

Electrician

Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: 10th Pass
Seats: 20 (General) | 10 (SC/ST)

Training in house wiring, industrial wiring, electrical machines, wiring for commercial buildings, and maintenance of electrical equipment.

Fitter

Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: 10th Pass
Seats: 20 (General) | 10 (SC/ST)

Assembly, fitting, and manufacturing of mechanical parts. Includes reading engineering drawings, drilling, tapping, and precision measurement.

Mechanic Diesel

Duration: 1 Year
Eligibility: 10th Pass
Seats: 24 (General) | 12 (SC/ST)

Repair and maintenance of Diesel engines used in vehicles, generators, and agricultural machinery.

Welder (G & W)

Duration: 1 Year
Eligibility: 8th Pass
Seats: 20 (General) | 10 (SC/ST)

Training in Gas Welding, Arc Welding, MIG Welding, TIG Welding for fabrication of metal structures.

Turner

Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: 10th Pass
Seats: 20 (General) | 10 (SC/ST)

Operation of Lathe machines to shape metal and wood. Includes threading, grooving, and precision turning operations.

Machinist

Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: 10th Pass
Seats: 20 (General) | 10 (SC/ST)

Advanced machining operations using Milling, Grinding, and CNC machines to produce precision components.

COPA

Duration: 1 Year
Eligibility: 10th Pass
Seats: 24 (General) | 12 (SC/ST)

Computer Operator & Programming Assistant. MS Office, Tally, Internet, and Basic Programming.

Sewing Technology

Duration: 1 Year
Eligibility: 8th Pass
Seats: 20 (General) | 10 (SC/ST)

Garment making, cutting, tailoring, embroidery, and dress designing. (Mostly for Women candidates).
